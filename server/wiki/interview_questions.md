# Interview Questions & Prep — AI / ML / Data Science

A structured reference for interview preparation in the AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science domains.

---

## 🤖 GenAI / LLM Interview Questions

**Common Questions:**
- What is the difference between Pre-training and Fine-tuning in LLMs?
- How does the self-attention mechanism work in Transformers?
- Explain the role of Positional Encoding in Transformers.
- What is the difference between Top-k and Nucleus (Top-p) sampling?
- How do you reduce hallucination in LLMs?
- Explain the concept of PEFT (Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning) and LoRA.

**Interview Tips:**
- Focus on practical trade-offs (e.g., latency vs. accuracy, cost vs. quality).
- Be prepared to discuss deployment challenges and optimization techniques like quantization.

---

## 📚 RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) Interview

**Common Questions:**
- Explain the complete RAG pipeline step-by-step.
- What are vector embeddings and why are they crucial for RAG?
- How do you evaluate a RAG system? (e.g., RAGAS framework metrics like faithfulness, answer relevance).
- What are some advanced RAG techniques? (e.g., query expansion, HyDE, re-ranking, maximal marginal relevance (MMR)).
- How do you handle chunking for a RAG system effectively?

---

## 💼 Data Science & Classical ML Interview Questions

**Common Questions:**
- Explain the bias-variance tradeoff.
- What is the difference between L1 (Lasso) and L2 (Ridge) regularization?
- How does a Random Forest differ from Gradient Boosting?
- How do you handle imbalanced datasets? (e.g., SMOTE, class weights).
- Explain cross-validation and why it's used.
- What metrics would you use to evaluate a classification model vs. a regression model?
