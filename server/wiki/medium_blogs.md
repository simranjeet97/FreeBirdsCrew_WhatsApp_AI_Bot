## About the Blog

Simranjeet writes technical articles on Medium covering Generative AI, Large Language Models, RAG systems, Agentic AI, and practical machine learning. The blog targets ML engineers, data scientists, and AI enthusiasts who want to go beyond surface-level tutorials and understand the implementation details.
