# Research Papers — AI / ML / GenAI

A compilation of crucial research papers and their summaries in the Artificial Intelligence space.

---

## 📜 Foundational Papers

**Attention Is All You Need (Vaswani et al., 2017)**
- **Concept:** Introduced the Transformer architecture, replacing RNNs/LSTMs with self-attention mechanisms.
- **Why it matters:** The foundation of all modern Large Language Models (LLMs) like GPT, Llama, and BERT.

**BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers (Devlin et al., 2018)**
- **Concept:** Bidirectional training of Transformers for language understanding.
- **Why it matters:** Revolutionized NLP tasks like classification, named entity recognition, and search.

---

## 🧠 Large Language Models (LLMs)

**Language Models are Few-Shot Learners (Brown et al., 2020) - GPT-3**
- **Concept:** Demonstrated that massive scaling coupled with next-word prediction leads to zero-shot and few-shot capabilities without explicit fine-tuning.

**Llama 2 / Llama 3 Series (Meta AI)**
- **Concept:** Open-weight models pushing the boundaries of what is possible with efficient architecture and intense alignment (RLHF).

---

## 📚 RAG & Information Retrieval

**Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Knowledge-Intensive NLP Tasks (Lewis et al., 2020)**
- **Concept:** The original paper introducing RAG, combining a pre-trained parametric memory (LLM) with a non-parametric memory (vector index of external documents).

**Precise Zero-Shot Dense Retrieval without Relevance Labels (HyDE) (Gao et al., 2022)**
- **Concept:** Hypothetical Document Embeddings (HyDE) - generating a fake answer using an LLM and using it for retrieval to improve semantic match.

---

## ⚙️ Fine-Tuning & Optimization

**LoRA: Low-Rank Adaptation of Large Language Models (Hu et al., 2021)**
- **Concept:** Freezes pre-trained model weights and injects trainable rank decomposition matrices into each layer, drastically reducing the number of trainable parameters.
